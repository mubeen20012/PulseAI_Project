## 🔬 1. Project Vision
PulseAI is a high-fidelity medical intelligence system designed to move beyond single-source diagnostics. By fusing tabular clinical data, 2D medical imaging (X-rays), and 1D time-series signals (ECG), it provides a holistic heart disease risk assessment.

## 🧠 3. Multi-Modal Architecture
The system uses a **Late Fusion** strategy to process data:

1. **ANN (Artificial Neural Network):** Handles 13 structured parameters (Age, BP, BMI, etc.).
2. **CNN (MobileNetV2):** Performs feature extraction on Chest X-ray images (224x224).
3. **LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory):** Processes temporal ECG signal sequences.
4. **Fusion Layer:** Concatenates embeddings into a unified Dense layer (256 units) for final probability calculation.

---

## 🔒 4. Engineering Features (v2.0)
- **Persistent Storage:** Every user has a profile saved in **SQLite**, tracking their risk history over time.
- **Session Security:** **Flask-Login** manages secure entry, protecting patient confidentiality.
- **Natural Language Interface:** An integrated **AI Chatbot** translates technical probabilities into human-readable health advice.
- **Cloud-Ready:** A `Dockerfile` ensures the project runs identically on Render, Hugging Face, or AWS.

---

## 📊 5. Risk Scoring Logic
The system outputs a probability score translated into actionable tiers:
- **🔴 High Risk (≥ 0.75):** Critical attention needed.
- **🟡 Moderate Risk (0.45 – 0.74):** Monitoring and lifestyle changes.
- **🟢 Low Risk (< 0.45):** Baseline maintenance.
